The Source

Karakoram Glacier

35°52'N 75°32'E — Northern Pakistan

Deep within the Karakoram range, where tectonic forces have sculpted the world's most dramatic peaks, lies a glacier that has been pure for 12,000 years.

At altitudes where no human civilization has ever settled, snow falls on untouched rock, compresses under its own weight, and transforms into ice of impossible clarity. This is where Exper begins.

We harvest only at the glacier's edge, where nature has already done what no technology can replicate—complete purification through time and pressure.
